Creating the Menu: Call AppMenu::createAppMenu(Format)
    1. AppMenu creates a MenuInitializer for Format.

    2. AppMenu passes the initializer to a new MainComponent.
        a. MainComponent creates the ConfigFile, initializing menu data.

        b. MainComponent uses the initializer to create a MenuComponent.
            I.  MenuComponent creates a new root FolderComponent.
                i.  FolderComponent creates new MenuButtons for its menu items.

                ii. FolderComponent adds and shows MenuButtons.

            II. MenuComponent adds and shows FolderComponent.

        c. MainComponent creates a new Controller, passing it the MenuComponent
           and the OverlaySpinner.
            I.  Controller stores a pointer to the MenuComponent and a reference
                to the OverlaySpinner.

        d. MainComponent creates a new InputHandler with the initializer,
           passing it the Controller and the MenuComponent.
            I.  InputHandler connects itself to the MenuComponent's mouse and
                key events.

            II. InputHandler stores a pointer to the MenuComponent.

            III.InputHandler stores a pointer to the Controller.

        e. MainComponent stores the Controller, InputHandler and MenuComponent 
           in unique_ptr objects.

        f. MainComponent adds and shows the MenuComponent as a child component.
    
    3. AppMenu returns the new MainComponent.
